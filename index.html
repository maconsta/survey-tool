Internet News - Emotion Survey

You will be asked to read five news items in English and then group together the ones that invoke similiar emotions in you. These items are published online by well known journals.

For the purposes of language-emotion analysis, you will be asked to specify your native language. The data collected from this survey will be statistically analysed to draw research conclusions in the domain of language communication and emotion. The survey is anonymous.
